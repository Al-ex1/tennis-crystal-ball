Latest New Features New!

  • Tournaments browser - Browse tournaments, see players with most titles, historical tournament levels and surfaces and average participation.
  • Top Performers and Statistics Leaders enhancements - Much more filters: by season, by tournament level, by surface, by round, vs rank, vs age, vs hand/backhand, vs seeding, by height, by tournament and by country.
  • Records 'GOAT' Points - 'GOAT' Points for selected records, see 'GOAT' Legend for the details
  • GOAT Points for the consecutive and repeating wins at the same Grand Slam tournament (i.e. 5 consecutive wins at Wimbledon = (5 - 1) * 1 = 4 points, 10 total wins at Roland Garros = (10 - 1) / 2 = 4.5 points).
  • Rankings table improvements - Ranking and ranking points movement columns are added to both ATP and Elo ranking tables. To see ranking and ranking points movers of the week (up and down), use sorting on these columns.
  • Player Season Summary - On player profile and head-to-head with season highlights and season performance breakdown on the glance
  • Weeks at Elo Top 5 'GOAT' Points - 'GOAT' Points for weeks at Elo Top 5 (see 'GOAT' Points Legend) to better reflect domination over fellow players in the same Era
